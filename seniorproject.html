The Chocoholics Guide to College Baking

Senior Project progress mockups

The Chocoholics Guide to College Baking is an interactive website that I built for my Senior Project. In May of 2024 I graduated from the University of Connecticut with a BFA in Digital Media and Design. My goal was to create a website that showcases the skills I have acquired during my time at UConn as well as something that represents me. I wanted my audience to look at the project and immediately be able to say “Gabi made that”. Baking is my favorite hobby so it was easy to make the decision to display my most baked recipes on a website that I both designed and developed. Since I have a concentration in Web and Interactive Media Design I created a high fidelity prototype before beginning the coding process. Above is the link to the like project. Enjoy!
