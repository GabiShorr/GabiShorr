Senior Project - Midterm Progress

Digital Products + Experiences

Project Plan: For my senior project I am making an interactive cookbook website that displays my favorite baked goods to make. It is geared towards college students baking in their limited college apartments. As a college student myself I have made these recipes many times and can give accurate tips and tricks on how to bake these recipes with limited supplies. To do this I am going to design mockups in Adobe Xd then code the project using HTML, CSS, and JS.

Targeted Users: College students looking to up their baking game- how to make the recipes in your college apartment

What makes my design different: My design is unique because it will have the features of a normal recipe website but it will incorporate a modern ux/ui design making it different from most other sites like it. It is also geared towards college students making it different from most other sites.

What kind of features will your design have and why do you add those features?: My design will feature carousels, animated text, various interactive hover states on text and images, as well as parallax images.
